Privacy Policy: Users' privacy is respected and safeguarded by the Sahay and Mochan mobile applications ("Apps"). These apps are only meant to collect the bare minimum of data that is typically required to perform AI-based mental health assessments for depression and anxiety. When a user initiates a screening session on their own, the apps may use the device's camera for a brief period of time to examine common facial expression patterns and obtain computational features that the AI model requires but cannot be linked to a particular individual. Raw photos or videos are not retained unless the user requests it; data processing is only intended to provide the user with wellness information. Gathering technical information such as device type, app usage logs, and anonymized performance metrics could improve the research and system's dependability. Secure encryption protocols safeguard data transferred between the user's device and the server, and data handling complies with all relevant privacy and data protection regulations. The apps are limited to research, early screening, and health monitoring. The collected data will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties unless required by law or the user consents. Users are free to stop using the apps at any moment. They accept this Privacy Policy and its associated Terms and Conditions by continuing to use the Apps.
